  • Biography

  • Blake Hose is a 23 year old ultra-trail runner based in Geelong, Australia. With results befitting veterans of the sport, Blake is still new to the sport and only just getting started. With a podium place on debut at Transvulcania 2015, this kid has the whole world at his feet. Literally.
